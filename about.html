ABOUT ME

Sports and Video Games

From an early age I loved to be active. Whether that meant riding my bike with friends or playing sports, I always had to be moving. Once I was able to participate in organized sports, that was my entire life. I started trying out most sports being baseball, soccer, basketball, soccer and last but not least hockey. I ended up playing hockey and soccer the most until I ultimately choose to play hockey. I started playing at the age of 3 and I stopped last year at the age of 20. Though my career has finished as an athlete, I still love to watch clips of my favorite NHL players lobotomize their opponents. Pavel Datsyuk Clip

When I am not busy with school or from playing sports, I also enjoy playing video games. My go to games are GTA, Call of Duty and, my personal favorite, Rocket League. Rocket League can be simply described as car soccer but it is also much more complex than that. The game is filled with mechanically gifted players that are able to manipulate the balls movement with extreme precision. This allows the player to either pass to their teammate or be a one man show and score at will. It is fun to compete against others players of similar skill in the competitive game mode.
